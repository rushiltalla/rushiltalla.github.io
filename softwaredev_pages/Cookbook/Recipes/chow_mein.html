Chow Mein

Servings: 3 servings Time: 20 minutes

Equipment

  • Wok (preferably)
  • Gas Stove (preferably)
  • Pasta Strainer

Ingredients

  • 10 oz noodles (Hakubaku Udon)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cups shredded cabbage and carrots
  • 1/4 cup onions
  • 0.75 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 2 cloves garlic, diced
  • - Sauce
  • 3 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 0.5 tbsp lime juice
  • 3.5 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1.5 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp wheat flour
  • 0.5 tbsp sugar

Directions

  1. Add 1 tbsp oil to a large pan/wok on medium/high heat. Once hot, add ginger and garlic. Let cook for a minute, then add the onion. Add the rest of the vegetables (half of the cabbage mix), and stir frequently. Stir fry for 2-3 minutes and store for later.
  2. In a bowl, mix together the sauce.
  3. Cook the noodles as instructed. Do not over cook, as it will be cooked again with the sauce. Once the noodles are done, rince with cold water to wash off the starch.
  4. Add the second tbsp of oil to the pan/wok on medium/high heat. Add the noodles and stir fry for 2 minutes. Add the sauce and stir fry for another 2 minutes. Finally, add the vegetables and remaining raw cabbage mix and cook for another 2 minutes.
