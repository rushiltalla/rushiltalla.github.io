Whole Wheat Bread

Servings: 8 servings Time: 90 minutes

Equipment

  • Bread Loaf Pan

Ingredients

  • 3 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1/6 cup vital wheat gluten
  • 2 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1.25 cups very warm water (120 F)
  • 1/2 tbsp salt
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tsp lemon juice

Directions

  1. In a medium bowl, add the yeast to the water and mix. Cover and allow yeast to rest.
  2. Mix 2 cups of the wheat flour and all of the wheat gluten together well.
  3. Add the yeast mixture to the flour and combine using a stand mixer.
  4. After it looks mixed, cover with a damp cloth/towel and allow the dough to rest for 10 minutes.
  5. Add the salt, olive oil, honey, and lemon juice and combine. Slowly add the remaining whole wheat flour as you mix (preferably with a stand mixer).
  6. Preheat the oven to 350°F, wait for 1 minute, and turn it off. This is to create a warm environment for the dough to rise.
  7. After the dough is well kneaded, add to a parchment paper lined bread loaf pan. Gently press the dough so it fills the bottom of the pan.
  8. Move to the warmed oven and allow to rise for 50 minutes. Keep the pan covered for 30 of those minutes, to ensure it does not dry out.
  9. Without removing the pan from the oven, set the oven to 350°F and bake for 35 minutes. The internal temperature of the bread should read 205°F.
  10. Remove from pan imediately to prevent condencation and allow to cool before cutting.
