Lasagna

Servings: 8 servings Time: 90 minutes

Equipment

  • 9x9 Baking Tray

Ingredients

  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 medium onion
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 10oz ricotta cheese
  • 8 oz baby bella mushrooms
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 0.5 tsp pepper
  • 0.5 tsp cumin
  • Marinara Sauce
  • 3 layers worth of lasagna noodles

Directions

  1. Boil some water and start cooking the lasagna noodles. Make sure they are not overcooked (otherwise will become too soft and break easily).
  2. Preheat oven to 375Â°F.
  3. Dice the vegatables finely and cook in the oil. At the end, add the ricotta cheese.
  4. In the 9x9 tray, add a layer of sauce.
  5. Add a layer of noodles.
  6. Add a layer of veg mix.
  7. Repeat layering two more times.
  8. Cook for 35 minutes.
