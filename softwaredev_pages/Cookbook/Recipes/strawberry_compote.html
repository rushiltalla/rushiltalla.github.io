Strawberry Compote

Servings: 6 servings Time: 20 minutes

Equipment

  • Saucepan

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs of strawberries, chopped
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 large pinch of salt

Directions

  1. Combine strawberries, maple syrup, lemon juice, vanilla, and salt in a small saucepan. Bring to a simmer over medium heat, stirring occasionally, for 15-18 minutes, or until the strawberries are soft.
  2. Remove from the heat, the compote will thicken as it cools.
