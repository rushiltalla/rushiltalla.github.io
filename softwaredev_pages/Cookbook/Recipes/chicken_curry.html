Chicken Curry

Servings: 8 servings Time: 90 minutes

Equipment

  • Blender

Ingredients

  • Chicken Marinade:
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1.75 lbs chicken
  • 1.5 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1.5 tsp chili powder
  • 3 tbsp greek yogurt
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/4 lime (juiced)
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • Curry:
  • 4 roma tomatoes
  • 2 medium onions
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 0.5 tsp cumin seeds
  • 0.5 tsp black mustard seeds
  • 0.5 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp salt

Directions

  1. In a medium bowl, mix together the chicken marinade. Let rest for at least 1 hour.
  2. Finely dice the tomatoes and onions.
  3. Heat the oil in a pan on medium heat. Roast the cumin seeds and black mustard seeds.
  4. Add the tomatoes and onions, along with the chili powder and salt. Let this cook until the onions are translucent.
  5. Blend the tomato and onion mixture so that it forms a paste.
  6. Add the chicken to the pan and cook for 5 minutes, make sure to stir/flip the pieces.
  7. Add the tomato and onion gravy and cook for around 10 more minutes (check that the chicken is fully cooked).
