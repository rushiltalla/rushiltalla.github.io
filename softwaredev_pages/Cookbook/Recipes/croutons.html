Croutons

Servings: 6 servings Time: 40 minutes

Equipment

  • Baking Tray
  • Two large bowls
  • Parchment paper

Ingredients

  • 8 cups of any bread, cut into cubes of preferred size
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1.5 tbsp italian seasoning
  • 1.5 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 0.75 tsp salt

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375Â°F.
  2. Line a large baking sheet with parchement paper.
  3. In a large bowl, evenly drizzle the olive oil over the bread chunks. Evenly sprinkle the seasonings over the bread.
  4. Toss the pieces until well-combined by placing another bowl as a lid over it and shaking.
  5. Spread the cubes out in a single layer over the baking sheet
  6. Bake until it achives desired crispness (probably 15-20 minutes), turning them after 10 minutes.
  7. Let cool before using or storing in an air-tight container.
