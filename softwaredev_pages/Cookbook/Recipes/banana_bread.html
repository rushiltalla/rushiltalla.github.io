Banana Bread

Servings: 1 loaf Time: 65 minutes

Equipment

  • Loaf Pan
  • Rubber Spatula

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 3 large ripe bananas
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
  2. In a bowl, add butter and peeled bananas. Mash with a fork.
  3. Add vanilla extract and egg, stir until no yellow streaks of egg remain.
  4. In a separate large bowl, mix the flour, sugar, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon.
  5. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix together until combined.
  6. Pour the batter into the loaf pan and sprinkle chocolate chips on top.
  7. Bake for 45-55 minutes, until poke test in center comes out clean.
  8. Let it sit until cool.
