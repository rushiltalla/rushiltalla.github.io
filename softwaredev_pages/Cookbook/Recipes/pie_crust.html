Pie Crust

Servings: 2 Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together the butter and sugars until smooth.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  4. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt; gradually blend into the creamed mixture.
  5. Stir in the chocolate chips by hand using a wooden spoon.
  6. Drop by rounded spoonfuls onto ungreased baking sheets.
  7. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes, or until golden brown.
