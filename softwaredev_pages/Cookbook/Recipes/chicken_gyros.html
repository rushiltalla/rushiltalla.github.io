Chicken Gyros

Servings: 4 servings Time: 45 minutes

Equipment

  • Pan

Ingredients

  • - Chicken
  • 1 lb chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 3 garlic cloves, diced
  • 1 tbsp oregano
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 cup greek yogurt
  • 1.5 tbsp olive oil
  • - Gyro
  • Pita Bread
  • Chicken/Tofu
  • Breakfast Potatoes
  • Tzatziki
  • Coleslaw Mix
  • Lime Juice

Directions

  1. Mix the chicken marinade together. Add the chicken and let rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Cook the chicken on medium/low heat (to prevent drying, flip only once! Flip when the white "cooked" section reaches half way up.).
  3. After letting the chicken rest for 7 minutes, cut into strips.
  4. Assemble the Gyro with all of the components.
