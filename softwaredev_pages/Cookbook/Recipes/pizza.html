Pizza

Servings: 2 pizzas Time: 30 minutes

Equipment

  • 2 Pizza Pans
  • Large Bowl
  • Aluminum Foil

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/3 cups warm water (between 100-110°F)
  • 2 and 1/4 tsp instant yeast (1 packet)
  • 1.5 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1.5 tsp salt
  • 3.5 cups bread flour (or wheat flour)
  • Onions, garlic, parmesan, mozerella, chicken, tomatoes as toppings

Directions

  1. Combine the warm water, yeast, and sugar in a small bowl. Cover and allow to rest for 5 minutes.
  2. Add the flour, salt, and olive oil to a large bowl and mix.
  3. Add the yeast mixture to the large bowl and mix until combined. Then, oil your hand and knead the dough, adding extra flour if the dough becomes too sticky (or warm water if too dry). Check if you can stretch a ball of the dough into a thin film you can see light through without it tearing to know when it is ready.
  4. Grease the large bowl with an oil coating, then return the dough and cover with foil. Allow dough to rise in a warm place for 90 minutes (in oven with light on, can heat oven slightly as well).
  5. Preheat oven to 475°F
  6. Punch the dough down to release air bubbles. Cut it into two pieces, and stretch them out into disks slightly. Then return them to the bowl and cover again so they can rest while you prepare the toppings.
  7. With the toppings ready, take out the dough and flatten onto the pizza pans, stretching without breaking the dough. Poke center region with a fork to prevent bubbles from forming in the pizza. Add sauce and toppings.
  8. Bake in oven for around 13 minutes.
  9. Immediately after removing, optionally apply a parmesan garlic oil mixture around the crust.
