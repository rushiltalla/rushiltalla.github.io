Apple Blueberry Strawberry Pie

Servings: 12 Time: 90 minutes

Equipment

  • 9-in pie dish

Ingredients

  • 2 pie dough disks
  • 4 cups apples, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 2 cups blueberries
  • 1 cup strawberries, sliced
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • pinch of salt
  • (optional) 1 egg + 1 tsp milk for eggwash

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Remove a pie dough disk from the fridge, roll it out to fit the size of the pie dish.
  3. Carefully lay the rolled dough onto the pie dish, allowing it to fall into place as it starts to warm. Put it back in the fridge to keep it cold.
  4. Take out the other pie disk, and roll it out to be 2 inch larger than the pie disk. Cut it into 1 in wide strips, then put back in fridge.
  5. In a large mixing bowl, combine the apples, blueberries, strawberries, sugar, lemon zest, lemon juice, cornstarch, cinnamon, and salt and mix well.
  6. If there is excess liquid from the fruit mixture, pour and reduce the liquid in a pan until it thickens.
  7. Add the fruit mixture to the chilled pie crust.
  8. Layer the dough strips above the pie to form a lattice.
  9. Brush the crust with egg wash and sprinkle with coarse sugar.
  10. Bake in bottom third of the oven at 400°F for 20 min, then 375°F for about 30 additional min, or until pie is browned and bubbly.
  11. Allow pie to cool completely before slicing and serving.
